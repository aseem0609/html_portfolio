Movie watched by Aseem Anand in theater

2018

This was the first time I went to a movie theater. My first movie experience will probably be the best movie experience.

1. Avengers : Infinity War

This is probably the best Avengers movie if not the best (behind Endgame). The experience when Thor arrives in Wakanda, I was literally shaking while sitting on my seat. This is my best ever theater experience and will probably be always be. Everything in the movie is good, the story, the pace, the build up for the next and final movie of phase 3. Absolute masterpiece. My rating for this movie will be 9.5/10

2023

1. Pathan

This was SRK's first movie since his 5 year break from BOLLYWOOD. For me it was average but people liked it and the movie grossed 1000cr+. The hype and craziness was unreal in theater. The movie did create controversy but overall rating from me will be 6/10.

2. Ant man and the Wasp: Quantumania

This was an absolute crazy movie. They explored the Quantum world. I enjoyed the movie very much and the post credit scene is also awesome. I already had spoilers about the post credit scene but still I shouted very loud when I saw the post credit scene. The movie was also awesome. The creatures which was shown in Quantum realm was interesting and crazy af. The real fear of Kang was shown in the movie. But the critics and other audience didn't like it very much so it was not so hit like other Marvel movies. One of the imporant movie from the trip to theater would be that I was bitten by a very large and scary ANT after we came out of theater and my friends and me were joking that now I would become ANTMAN. The sting was a bit painful though. My rating 7.5/10.

3. Evil Dead Rise

There is nothing special in this movie. Its more gross than being Scary. My rating 5.5/10
